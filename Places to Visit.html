Gangani; The grand canyon of Bengal

One of the most enchanting places at the very heart of Garhbeta where Nature has flourished into her most beautiful form. Naturally, it attracts diverse wildlife hence photography freaks. Legends dictate it is the very place where Pancha Pandavas took shelter during their years in exile & Bhima slaughtered the Bakrakshasa. The place is famous for its prismatic view of the sunrise and sunset. Besides, the place is a registered tourist and picnic spot.

Placeholder Gangani

Sharba Mangala Mandir

The infamous temple of Garhbeta is a sacred place for spirituality and purity for devotees of the Deity Sharba Mangala from different corners of the area. People come here seeking blessings and praying for the wellbeing of their family & the people they love. Sometimes newly bought vehicles also find their first visit here before they officially hit the road.
